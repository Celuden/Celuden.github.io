Chess Bot

Programming Python Algorithms

Project Description

One task during my studies was the creation of a chess bot to compete in a local tournament between students to determine the best chess bot. We created a bot account on Lichess.com and programmed our bot using python. During the tournament itself, my bot "FireflyLullaby" placed second with a strategy of stalling out games and playing quickly, so the game time will run out for the opposing bot.

While developing the bot itself, some algorithms such as Minimax or Alpha-Beta-Pruning were programmed from scratch so the runtime of the bot would be fitting for a chess bot. Different strategies such as the opening book were also implemented to mimic professional chess players which utilize statistical success of the first moves in a chess game. My bot only predicted up to 5 turns to decide on which move is the best to stay within a thinking time limit of 3 seconds - a higher search depth would also have been possible to use at the cost of a higher runtime and requiring a higher thinking time limit to be fully utilized.
