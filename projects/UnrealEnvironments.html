Project Description

Experiencing the neverending dunes of the golden sands, strolling through the flower field at midnight and exploring the depths of a dark cave system - all these are feelings I longed to capture with the environments I created. The projects featured on this page, "Pyrite Sands", "At Midnight" and "Clinging To Hope", were all created in Unreal Engine 5 and rendered via the Movie Render Queue - these projects were primarily created using assets from Fab and the post-production of the renders was done in After Effects.

All environments have an underlying theme and feelings they want to convey to the watcher, ranging from darker themes such as in Clinging To Hope, to lighter relaxing ones like in Pyrite Sands. Adding camera tracking shots improves the mood and setting of each environment, further highlighting the look and aesthetics as well as making the scenery feel more alive. In addition to the strategic placement of Fab assets, I also created fitting visual and material effects such as foliage wind and scene particles to improve upon the final look of the environment.
