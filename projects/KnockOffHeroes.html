Knock-Off Heroes

Game C++ SFML 2D

Project Description

In "Knock-Off Heroes" you can choose between four playable heroes to fend off the evil that is reigning in the world. Craboo, KweenB, Toxicalyptus and Flop4925 need to work together as a team to conquer the evil mayhem that is unfolding. Yet somehow all of them do not seem to be team players and start attacking each other - what unfolds is a battle for might and glory. "Knock-Off Heroes" is a local 4-player PVP/PVE experience where you fend off waves of enemies whilst also sabotaging other players. You gain points by defeating enemies and other players alike and can even use your special ability to steal points from them!

This project was the first team project during my bachelor studies and was solely created through C++ code using SFML for hardware bindings. I was responsible for programming the individual characters and the combat experience as well as creating and managing the assets for the game. The project was also our submission for the MultiMediaProject 2a in the third semester of our studies as well as our start for implementing game systems such as physics, pooling, collision handling and so on.
