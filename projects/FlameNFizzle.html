Flame 'N' Fizzle

Game C# Unity 3D GameJam

Project Description

Flame 'n' Fizzle is a game where a fire slime has to navigate through the harsh slime world and avoid water on its way down towards the earth core. A friendly ghost guardian angel slime has to help the fire slime in order for it to survive the obstacles on the way. By eating up the items from the upper platforms and spitting them out down below, the fire slime can stay healthy and these items also have additional effects that affect the slime in different ways so it can pass through the hole in the middle of the map to proceed.

This project was a GameJam project in a decently sized team, where we had a 48 hour timeframe to create a game to the theme of "fire". I was mainly responsible for programming menus, UI, the two-player-controllers and the level handling, as well as managing the programmer-side of the project.
