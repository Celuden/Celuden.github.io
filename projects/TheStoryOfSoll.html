The Story of Söll

Game C# Unity 2D

Project Description

My first time using Unity was when creating a game for my diploma project titled "The Story of Söll" - which was a game developed to actively deliver the message of the importance of climate change. In the game you play as Ivan the hedgehog and experience his life throughout the seasons with gameplay elements such as jumping and crouching to support its platformer game genre as well as having worm collectibles for completing the main objective. Enemies are represented as owls hunting for the hedgehog, trying to hinder you on your quest to support your family and get through the harsh winter.

This was my first interaction with coding and scripting in Unity C# and the game itself has a playtime of around half an hour with some cutscenes for storytelling. Everything in the game, including the programming, art and music, as well as SFX, were created solely by me. Youtube tutorials were used in some instances to learn the basics of coding and working with Unity. The game is only available in german.
