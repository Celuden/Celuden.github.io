The Dark Place

Game RPG Maker VX Ace 2D

Project Description

For my very first content creation project, back in 2015, I started the development of a simple RPG Maker game called "The Dark Place". The game was completed in early 2016 and was not publicly released on any platforms - it was just a project to play for myself and my inner friend circle due to its simple and silly nature. English was the main language of the game and since it was pretty early on in my life, speaking or writing another language besides german was not my strong suit, especially regarding grammar.

This project came to be due to the fact that I wanted to create my very own story and characters and realize them with some gameplay elements. Most art content was provided by the engine from the get go and only the content itself such as gameplay stats, items, story and fights were designed and programmed by me.
