Knastbruch

Game C# Unity 2D GameJam

Project Description

In Knastbruch, english title "Prison Escape", you are a prisoner that attempts to escape from prison while avoiding several different obstacles on your path to freedom. These obstacles include teachers trying to argue with you, homeless people begging for your belongings or trash on the ground that you have to correctly recycle - nothing that you can ignore on your way home. Avoid being spotted by the prison's spotlights and get to the escape truck in time!

This project marks my first time participating in a GameJam with the theme of "light" in a team of 5 - we had 48 hours to complete and submit the game for evaluation and got second place. As the sole developer I was mainly responsible for creating all code-related features of the game in Unity with C# scripting and implement all the assets created by my team members.
