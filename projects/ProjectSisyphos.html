Project: Sisyphos

Game C# Unity 3D

Project Description

In "Project: Sisyphos" you try to escape from a spaceship that has been infiltrated by an unknown entity. You have a limited amount of time to solve all the puzzles and unlock the escape pod in order to escape your personal space coffin. Will you be able to handle the proposed challenges before your personal life clock runs out? Your goal is to find three batteries in order to power up the escape pod and to escape. You will have to solve several puzzles and mechanisms to access said batteries before the unknown entity reaches your location and does unspeakable things to your mortal shell.

This project was another team project and our first time using an engine to develop a game. Utilizing Unity with C# scripts, we worked on a smaller scoped space puzzle horror game, where I was responsible for programming the character controller as well as the puzzle system with each individual puzzle along with managing assets and implementing a fitting sound design for our setting. Project: Sisyphos was the submission for the MultiMediaProject 2b in the fourth semester of our studies.

Key Features

  • Otherwordly Endeavors

    Explore the space station and escape within the time limit to ensure your survival from the alien boarding the ship

  • Time is Ticking

    You have 10 minutes to solve all the puzzles atop the ship - can you do it?

  • Colorful Space Station

    Your space station is infected with a virus and only your custom-made flashlight can reveal the ship for what it truly is

  • Horror Setting

    With two different entities aboard the ship, you are thrown into a horror scenario where time is against you and your survival
