Realm Of Unrest

Game C++ Blueprints Unreal Engine 3D

Project Description

"Realm of Unrest" is an immersive first-person horror adventure game that combines the mystical arts of alchemy and necromancy with modern university life. In this game, players are thrust into a chilling narrative as a group of students who unintentionally unleash an evil entity during their group studies. The game revolves around their harrowing journey to rectify their error, delve into the dark mysteries of their academic institution, and ultimately confront the evil entity they've unwittingly set free. Complex puzzles and an immersive gameplay experience with various challenges and obstacles await those who dare to explore the Realm of Unrest. Will it be you or the entity that returns from the vast unknown void known as the Ether Realm?

In conjunction with the game project, a 3D animation film, “Echoes from Beyond”, was created to visualize the story of what happened before the game’s narrative. The videos are spread throughout the game and need to be unlocked while progressing. Discover Davian’s research and his actions that led to the current game story events. Additionally, a web project titled “Realm of Unrest: Web Experience” was developed for marketing purposes, promoting the game, and providing more content for players to explore. This project marks a collaboration of three different sub-projects, melded together into one sharp ether blade.

This was the bachelor project of our team so we went all out and combined three different projects to create one big world and story. I was the lead developer, game designer and producer of our team and handled most affairs that were revolving around the game project. My work primarily consisted of programming game systems such as gameplay, puzzles and game mechanics, UI programming, implementing assets, leading the project's direction, designing puzzle elements and providing feedback for story elements and implementation. The game project was created in Unreal Engine, with the film being pre-rendered and shown as a final result within the game and the website as a marketing tool to promote the world.
